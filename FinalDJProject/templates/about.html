About This Project

This investigation will highlight the number
of COVID-19 hospitalizations in New York City
particularly by checking the rates in each of the five boroughs
from February 29th, 2020 to May 5th, 2025.

The image below was my prototype for my macro
page that focuses on the entirety of
New York City

The image to the right was my prototype for my micro
page that focuses on the individual boroughs of
New York City

Feedback:

From peer feedback regarding the prototypes below I recieved three main critiques
1) I should add more eyecatching colors to the heatmap on my macro page
2) I should add more unique information to the micro page rather than re-iterating the borough from the macro map
3) There is no reason to repeat the date on the title of the page if the user manually selects it.

Takeaways:

From the feedback I received I learned that I should focus on making my macro page more visually appealing
and that I should focus on making my micro page more unique than my macro page.
